Math.AP: Analysis of PDEs

Mathematics of Active Gels: Bifurcations & Stability

April 9, 2020 8:30pm UTC
April 9, 2020 10:30pm CEST (Barcelona, Paris)
April 9, 2020 4:30pm EDT (New York, Boston)
April 9, 2020 1:30pm PDT (Los Angeles)
April 9, 2020 4:30pm UTC -04 (Local Time)
Speaker: Leonid V Berlyand (Penn State University) - Hosted by Princeton University

Abstract:

In this talk we present analytical studies of active gels and cell motility. We start from a brief overview of out-of-equilibrium active matter systems (e.g., active gels, bacterial suspensions, etc.) and mathematical challenges in their modeling and analysis. Next we present three minimal models of active gels: a phase-field model and two free boundary models. (...)
